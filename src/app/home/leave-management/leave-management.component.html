Why Leave management is Important

  • Optimizes workforce planning and resource allocation.
  • Ensures fair and consistent leave policies across the organization.
  • Reduces absenteeism and unplanned leave, improving productivity.
  • Facilitates compliance with labor regulations and company policies.
  • Streamlines leave request and approval processes, saving time.
  • Enhances employee satisfaction by providing transparency and flexibility.
  • Improves HR efficiency by automating leave tracking and management.
