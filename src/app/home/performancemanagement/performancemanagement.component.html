Why Performance management is Important

  • Drives employee development and growth, enhancing overall performance.
  • Aligns individual goals with organizational objectives for increased productivity.
  • Provides valuable feedback to employees, fostering continuous improvement.
  • Identifies top performers and areas for improvement, optimizing talent management.
  • Enhances communication and transparency between managers and employees.
  • Supports fair and objective assessment of employee contributions.
  • Increases employee engagement and job satisfaction through recognition and rewards.
