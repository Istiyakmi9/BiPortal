Why Attendance management is Important

  • Enhances workforce productivity by ensuring punctuality and accountability.
  • Facilitates payroll accuracy, reducing errors and overtime costs.
  • Helps in identifying attendance patterns and trends for better resource planning.
  • Improves compliance with labor laws and regulations.
  • Enables efficient scheduling and shift management.
  • Supports remote work arrangements by tracking virtual attendance.
  • SProvides data for performance evaluations and rewards based on attendance.
